How commissions for fursuits work:
  • I have flat rate pricing! Any species (no seriously, send me your birds, bugs and scalies). Any markings that can be hand/machine sewn and up to 5 fur colors. (additional fur colors will be +$25 per color)
  • Message me (Instagram, Discord: @inevitablegoose, Telegram: @xXinevitablegooseXx). Please put in your first message that you are interested in a commission. I will review your character or concept, select the commissions I'm interested in, and figure out details.
  • I will commit to 1-2 projects for every slot (a 3 month period). I usually commit to projects a slot in advance.
  • Payment for materials is due when I start on a project. For the rest of the amount, either payment in full or a payment plan will be arranged.
  • Projects are completed within 3 months of the start date. Progress pictures and work updates are provided regularly, and feedback from the client is taken into consideration.
  • When the suit is finished and fully paid for, it is shipped out for you to enjoy!
